I've realized there are certain requirements for future proofing oneself and posterity.
These requirements center on 1) managing one's health and well-being, 2) honing in-demand and timeless skills, 3) developing the ability to find, build, and maintain meaningful connections, and 4) actively passing these skills to the next generation.
